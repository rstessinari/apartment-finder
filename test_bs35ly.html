Description

CJ Hole Southville are delighted to welcome to the sales market this three bedroom, semi-detached home in Bedminster

This semi-detached property offers family accommodation by the way of a front porch, a cosy living room, a contemporary fitted kitchen/breakfast room, a downstairs WC and a spacious conservatory with patio doors providing access to a low maintenance, sunny garden. Upstairs hosts a master bedroom featuring a large wardrobe, a second double bedroom, an office room/bedroom and a generous sized, modern bathroom. Other benefits include a large shed perfect for storage and off-street parking.
This property is ideally situated within a quiet residential road in BS3 and is in close proximity of the metro-link, Bristol link road and Parsons Street train station providing excellent transport links. A vast array of well-respected schools, open green spaces and local amenities such as shops, pubs and cafes are within walking distance. There is easy access into Bristol City Centre making it fantastic location for commuters, keen shoppers and access to further transport links including Temple Meads Train Station and the M32.
This property is welcomed to the sales market with no onward chain.
